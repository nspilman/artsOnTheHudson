Bringing the Arts to Hudson County
For 38 Years

Arts on the Hudson works with the community to enrich the cultural life of Jersey City, NJ.

We have assembled a team of local residents and artists to produce events, classes, and multimedia content to help promote artists and arts organizations.

We provide platforms for a range of voices and perspectives as to reflect Jersey City's diverse and culterally rich community.

  • Our Team

How We Do It

Arts on the Hudson promotes the arts in our community through three main avenues - Early Childhood Arts Education, Events and Promotional Media.

Education

Arts on the Hudson provides arts education to the youth of Jersey City through partnership with the Headstart Program, provide early childhood arts education at no cost.






Events

From concerts to festivals, Arts on the Hudson brings the community together through events around town. Connect with local artists - we promise a good time.

Promotional Media

We provide platforms for a range of voices and perspectives as to reflect the diverse community that is Jersey City with low cost promotional media to help connect and promote the work of local artists and art organizations.
